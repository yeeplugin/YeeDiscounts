How to Set Up Free Shipping

The Free Shipping rule allows you to grant free shipping to customers when they meet certain criteria, such as a minimum order amount or purchasing specific products.

1. Creating a New Rule

  1. Navigate to WooCommerce > Settings > Dynamic Discounts.
  2. Click Add New Rule.
  3. Enter a Rule Name (e.g., "Free Shipping over $50").

2. Configuring the Discount

In the Discount Type section, select:

Discount Type: Free Shipping

Setting Up Conditions (The Trigger)

Define when Free Shipping should be applied.

  1. Scroll to the Condition section.
  2. Select a condition, usually:
    • Cart Subtotal: For "Spend X get Free Shipping".
    • Cart Items: For "Buy X product get Free Shipping".
  3. Example: Set Cart Subtotal >= 50.

3. Common Examples

Scenario A: Free Shipping on Orders Over $50

  • Discount Type: Free Shipping
  • Condition: Cart Subtotal >= 50
  • Result: If the cart total is $50+ (after other discounts), shipping costs are removed.

Scenario B: Free Shipping when buying a specific product

  • Discount Type: Free Shipping
  • Condition: Cart Item Quantity (of specific product) >= 1.
  • Result: Buying that product triggers free shipping for the whole order.
Note: Ensure your WooCommerce Shipping Zones are configured correctly. This rule typically overrides costs or interacts with existing shipping methods.
