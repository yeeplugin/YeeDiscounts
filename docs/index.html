Welcome to YeeDiscounts Documentation

YeeDiscounts is a powerful dynamic pricing and discounts plugin for WooCommerce. Create bulk discounts, BOGO deals, cart rules, and more with ease.

Available Guides

Discount Types

  • Buy X Get X (BOGO) →

    Create classic Buy One Get One Free deals or percentage discounts on the same product.

  • Buy 2 Get 3rd 50% Off →

    Setup volume discounts where buying more items unlocks a discount on the next item.

  • Simple Discount →

    Apply simple percentage or fixed price discounts to products or categories.

  • Bulk / Quantity Discount →

    Tiered pricing based on quantity purchased (e.g., Buy 10+ get 10% off).

  • Buy X Get Y →

    Purchase specific product(s) to get a discount on a different product.

  • Set / Bundle Discount →

    Fixed price or discount when buying a specific set of items together.

  • Cart Total Discount →

    Discounts based on the total value or quantity of the entire cart.

  • Free Shipping →

    Grant free shipping based on cart subtotal or other conditions.
