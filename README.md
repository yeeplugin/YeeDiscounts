# YeeDiscounts - Dynamic Pricing & Discounts for WooCommerce

**Boost your store sales with powerful, flexible, and easy-to-use discount rules.**

YeeDiscounts allows you to create complex pricing deals like "Buy 1 Get 1 Free", "Bulk Quantity Discounts", "Cart Total Discounts", and much more without writing a single line of code. Designed for speed and compatibility.

## üöÄ Key Features

### 1. Buy X Get X (BOGO)
Create classic BOGO deals to move inventory fast.
*   **Example**: Buy 1 T-Shirt, Get 1 Free.
*   **Recursive**: Repeats for every X items (Buy 2 Get 2, Buy 3 Get 3).
*   **Flexible Rewards**: Give the same item for free or at a % discount.

### 2. Buy X Get Y
Cross-sell products by rewarding customers with different items.
*   **Example**: Buy a Laptop, get a Wireless Mouse for free.
*   **Example**: Buy a Hoodie, get a Cap at 50% off.

### 3. Bulk & Tiered Pricing
Encourage larger orders with quantity-based discounts.
*   **Example**: Buy 5+ items get 10% off, Buy 10+ items get 20% off.
*   **Display Table**: Automatically show a pricing table on the product page.

### 4. Set / Bundle Discounts
Sell specific products together for a special price.
*   **Example**: Buy Shirt + Pants + Shoes for a fixed price of $99.
*   **Example**: Buy Shampoo + Conditioner and get 20% off both.

### 5. Cart Adjustments
Apply discounts based on the total value or quantity of the entire cart.
*   **Example**: "Spend $100, Get $10 Off".
*   **Example**: "Buy 5 items total, get 5% off the whole order".

### 6. Free Shipping
Grant free shipping dynamically based on subtotal or specific products in the cart.

### 7. Product Adjustment (Simple Discount)
Quickly put specific products or categories on sale without complex rules.
*   **Percentage**: 20% off all Hoodies.
*   **Fixed Amount**: $5 off every T-Shirt.

## üé® Smart Display Features
*   **Auto-Strikeout Prices**: Shows the discounted price (e.g., ~~$50~~ $40) on Shop and Product pages.
*   **Sale Badges**: Customizable "Sale!" badges. Include the percentage discount (e.g., "-20%") inside the badge.
*   **Quantity Table**: Show tiered pricing tables directly on product pages.
